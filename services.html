Diet Plan

A diet plan is a structured approach to nutrition designed to help you achieve specific fitness goals, whether it's weight loss, muscle gain, or improving overall health. At Fit-India, we believe that proper nutrition is the cornerstone of a successful fitness journey.

A well-structured diet plan ensures you get the right nutrients in the right amounts, helping you stay healthy and achieve your fitness goals effectively.

This high-protein, balanced-carb, and nutrient-rich diet plan is designed to help maintain overall fitness, support muscle recovery, and keep energy levels high throughout the day.

1 glass of lukewarm water (optional: add lemon and honey) 5-7 soaked almonds and 1-2 walnuts for healthy fats and micronutrients

3-4 boiled eggs (for vegetarians: paneer bhurji or besan chilla)
1 roti or 2 brown bread toasts
1 cup of black coffee or green tea for metabolism support

1 whey protein shake (or 1 glass banana shake with peanut butter)
4-5 soaked dates and 1 banana for natural sugars and quick energy

1 bowl of dal/chicken/fish for protein
2 rotis or 1 bowl of brown rice
1 bowl of fresh salad (cucumber, tomato, carrot)
1 bowl of curd for digestion and gut health

1 cup of green tea or black coffee
1 handful of dry fruits OR 1 peanut butter toast

1 bowl of grilled paneer/chicken/fish
1 bowl of boiled vegetables or soup
1 roti (preferably multigrain chapati)

1 glass of warm milk (optional: add turmeric for anti-inflammatory benefits)
